Kim and Kanye Take North West to Fashion Show

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, March 6, 2024

Kim Kardashian West and her husband Kanye are in Paris for Fashion Week and they brought along a very special guest with them; daughter North West. The 15-month-old and her parents are in Paris for some of the big shows, and she was with them when they attended the Balenciaga show on Wednesday. There were some who wondered why the couple would take their young child to a fashion show, but it seems they felt they had a valid reason.

“She start to cry when we were walking out the door, and we couldn’t leave her. So, it was a spur-of-the-moment thing. But it was an amazing fashion show for her to be at for her first show,” said the new Mrs. West of her daughter’s presence at the show. The entire family wore all black so that they could match at the show, and little North put on her Yeezus shirt, and several other pieces which just so happened to be a part of her mom’s fashion line for kids.

The only thing the toddler was missing was the baby Balenciaga bag that belongs to her 2-year-old cousin, Penelope. The little tyke was a fashionista at the fashion show sporting Kardashian Kids clothes such as the black leatherette flared skirt and the leatherette panel leggings. It was an edgy, dark look for the toddler and she managed to rock it while she showed onlookers her newest talent. Little North is now walking, and she proudly showed off her skills entering and exiting the show. Now that she’s a little older and a little more mobile, her parents might start thinking about giving little North a brother or a sister. Kim recently opened up to Ellen DeGeneres about her desire to have a bunch of kids if her pregnancy hadn’t been so difficult on her.
