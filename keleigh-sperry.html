Keleigh Sperry: Everything You Need to Know About the Actress and Model

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Keleigh Sperry is an actress and model who recently starred as the bride in Taylor Swift’s music video for “I Bet You Think About Me (Taylor’s Version)”.

She is also the wife of actor Miles Teller, who played the groom in the same video.

But who is Keleigh Sperry, and what else do we know about her?

How old is Keleigh Sperry?

Keleigh Sperry was born on October 16, 1992, in Orange County, California, USA.

That makes her 31 years old as of 2023.

She is a Libra by zodiac sign.

Keleigh Sperry’s family and siblings

Keleigh Sperry comes from a large family.

She has six siblings: four sisters and two brothers.

Their names are Katie, Christie, Michelle, Kevin, Brian, and JP.

Her parents are John and Rosemary Sperry.

Keleigh Sperry’s spouse and how long have they been together?

Keleigh Sperry married Miles Teller on September 1, 2019, in Hawaii.

They had a romantic ceremony at the Ritz-Carlton Kapalua Hotel, surrounded by their family and friends.

The couple have been together since 2013 when they met at a Grammys afterparty for The Black Keys.

How did Miles Teller and Keleigh Sperry meet?

Miles Teller and Keleigh met at a Grammys afterparty for The Black Keys in 2013.

Miles told Vogue that he was attracted to Keleigh from the start, but he had to work hard to get her to dance with him.

He said, “I didn’t think it went well, but after a few more attempts, I got her to dance with me, and a week later, we went on a date. After we met, I knew she was the one.”

Keleigh Sperry’s net worth

Keleigh Sperry’s net worth is not publicly known, but it is estimated to be around $1 million.

She earns money from her modeling and acting gigs, as well as from endorsements and sponsorships.

Her husband, Miles Teller, has a net worth of $12 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Conclusion

Keleigh is more than just Miles Teller’s wife or Taylor Swift’s music video co-star.

She is a talented actress and model who has her own identity and career.

She is also a loving partner and a proud member of a big family.

Keleigh is someone you should keep an eye on in the future.
