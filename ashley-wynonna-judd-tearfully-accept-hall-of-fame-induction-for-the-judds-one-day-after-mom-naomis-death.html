Ashley &amp; Wynonna Judd Tearfully Accept Hall of Fame Induction for The Judds, One Day After Mom Naomi

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, April 30, 2024

The Judds were inducted into the Country Music Hall of Fame on Sunday (May 1), just one day after the death of Naomi Judd.

Naomi‘s daughters Wynonna and Ashley Judd were in attendance to accept the induction during the ceremony in Nashville, Tenn.

Click inside to find out what happened during the ceremony…

The Associated Press is reporting that Wynonna and Ashley tearfully accepted the induction together while holding onto each other. They recited a Bible verse together while on stage.

“I’m sorry that she couldn’t hang on until today,” Ashley said while crying.

The family revealed on Saturday that Naomi died at the age of 76 due to “the disease of mental illness.”

“Though my heart is broken, I will continue to sing,” Wynonna said. She and Ashley recited Psalm 23 while saying goodbye to their mother.

Read the statement that the family released to announce Naomi‘s death on Saturday.
