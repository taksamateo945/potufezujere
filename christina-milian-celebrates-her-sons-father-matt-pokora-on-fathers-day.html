CHRISTINA MILIAN CELEBRATES HER SONS FATHER, MATT POKORA, ON FATHERS DAY

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, July 4, 2024

Matt Pokora enjoyed a very special Father’s Day on Sunday (June 21). Christina Milian took to Instagram to celebrate her boyfriend who commemorated the day with his family.

“Dear Hubby, nothing brings me more joy than to see you live out your dream of being a Dad to your very own son,” Christina wrote to Matt. “To say you were ready would be an understatement,” Milian exclaimed.

“I’ve never seen a man look forward to changing EVERY stinky diaper like you and catching every moment Isaiah does something new,” Christina further doted on her son’s father. “I’m happy you can experience the best feeling God could bless us with,” the celebrity mom said. “#HappyFathersDay my love. You’re doing a great job.”

Christina Milian and Matt Pokora welcomed their son, Isaiah Pokora, in January. The couple’s little one came after an exuberant gender reveal party at which Matt leaped upon learning that he would father a son.

“Legacy on the way,” Matt shared with his Instagram fans. “What a blessing,” Christina Milian wrote on her social media page.

Isaiah Pokora is Christina Milian and Matt Pokora’s first child together. Christina also has a daughter named Violet Nash from her first marriage to The-Dream. Stay tuned for more celebrity father news!
