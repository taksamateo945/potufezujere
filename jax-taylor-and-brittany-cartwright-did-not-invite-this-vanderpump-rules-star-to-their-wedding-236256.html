Jax Taylor And Brittany Cartwright Did Not Invite This Vanderpump Rules Star To Their Wedding

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, February 16, 2024

Brittany Cartwright stopped by the set of Watch What Happens Live to discuss her upcoming wedding plans with host Andy Cohen. While fans cannot wait to watch Cartwright and Jax Taylor walk down the aisle, there is one Vanderpump Rules star who won’t be attending the ceremony.

The couple was joined on stage by several of their co-stars, including Ariana Madix, Stassi Schroeder, Lala Kent, Scheana Shay, Kristen Doute, and Katie Maloney-Schwartz. When asked who is invited to the big day, Cartwright revealed that everyone on set will be at the ceremony.

“Everyone on this stage is definitely invited,” she stated.

James Kennedy is one notable Vanderpump Rules star who wasn’t on the set and is not getting an invite to the wedding. Kennedy is currently at odds with just about all of his co-stars after fat-shaming Maloney on a recent episode of Vanderpump Rules.

Kenney also bashed the couple during a freestyle rapping session on the show, in which he insulted Taylor for cheating on his soon-to-be wife. Needless to say, Kennedy probably wouldn’t go the wedding even if he was invited.

With Kennedy on the outs, Taylor and Cartwright could not be happier about tying the knot later this year. According to Bravo TV, Taylor revealed that they booked a luxurious venue for the nuptials called the Kentucky Castle. He also assured his co-stars that the wedding will be something they’ll never forget.

“Can’t believe I am getting married in a castle that is all ours for three nights… Gonna be a weekend to remember!” he shared.
