Chris Brown Posts New Pics Of Both His Son And Daughter And They Resemble Him So Much!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 19, 2024

The star’s two kids look like his spitting image! Chris Brown took to social media to share two pics of daughter Royalty and newborn son, Aeko and it’s safe to say that they both resemble their father, perhaps more than in any other pic he’d ever shared of them!

In other words, it appears that the more they grow up, the more the kids become Chris Brown’s mini-me and fans are freaking out over this information!

It did not take long for Chris’ followers to gather in the comment section and gush over the similarities.

The singer captioned the snap that showed his baby boy ‘Mr. Brown,’ while the one that showed his 5 year old daughter reads: ‘Ms. Brown.’

The first photo is a close-up of the baby in a Nike shirt looking precious, his huge eyes identical to his dad’s.

As for Royalty, she looks so grown up and glamorous in her black and white snap, wearing a pearl headband with a bird in her hair.

And that’s not the only accessory as she is also rocking a pair of sparkly earrings!
