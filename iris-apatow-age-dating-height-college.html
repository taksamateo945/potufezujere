Iris Apatow is a young and sizzling actress best known for her works in the comedy movies like Knocked Up and Funny People. Her major television breakthrough came with the 2016-released Netflix series, Love.

How Old Is Iris Apatow? Age, Parents, Sister, Ethnicity, College

Iris Apatow is an 18-years-of-age actress born in 2002 in Los Banos, California, the United States. She blows her birthday candles on 12th October every year. Iris holds white ethnicity and her birth sign is Libra.

Talking about her family, she is among the two daughters of her parents who are related to the entertainment industry. Her father, Judd Apatow is an American filmmaker who founded Apatow Productions. Through the production house, he produced and directed movies like The 40-Year-old Virgin, Knocked Up, This Is 40, Funny People, and so on.

Moreover, Iris’ mother, Leslie Mann, is an actress best known for her role in the movies like The Cable Guy, George of the Jungle, Big Daddy, The 40-Year-Old Virgin, Knocked Up, 17 Again, Funny People, and so on. Her elder sister, Maude Apatow, age 23, is also an actress who has appeared in the movies like Knocked Up, Funny People, and This Is 40.

For her education, Iris attended Crossroads School for Arts & Sciences which is a private college preparatory school in Santa Monica, California.

Iris Apatow Career & Net Worth

Iris was just five years old when she landed her first acting break as Charlotte in the movie titled Knocked Up. The 2007 romantic comedy movie was directed by her own father and it also featured her mother and her elder sister.

Two years later, Apatow yet again shared the screen with her family appearing as Ingrid in the black comedy-drama film, Funny People. Later in 2012, she reprised the role of Charlotte in the sequel of the 2007 Knocked Up movie titled This Is 40.

Iris’ first television appearance came in the Netflix romantic comedy series, Love. She appeared as Arya Hopkins in the show from 2016 to 2018. Her next big project is in her father’s upcoming comedy film, The Bubble.

Though Iris Apatow’s professional earnings remain under review as of now, she basks on her father’s riveting net worth which swirls around $150 million.
