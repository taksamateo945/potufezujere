Who Is Zayden Banks, Lil Durk’s Son?

Much is yet to be learned about the identity of Zayden Banks’ paternal grandmother. We can only tell that the woman who is said to be a nurse in some quarters, gave birth to Zayden’s father Lil Durk on the 19th of October 1992.
