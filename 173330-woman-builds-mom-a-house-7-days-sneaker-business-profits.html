Sneaker Reseller Builds Mom House With R121K Earnings, TikTok Video Stuns Mzansi

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, April 14, 2024
  • A businesswoman showed her impressive income of over R121K in earnings from sneaker reselling
  • She posted a video montage on TikTok which included a picture of her standing next to stacks of shoe boxes, smiling proudly
  • The successful lady also disclosed that she used some of the money to build her mother a house that was erected in seven days

In the competitive world of sneaker reselling, one TikTok user turned heads by raking in a staggering R121K in earnings.

She also used the money to begin the construction of a new home for her mother and showed the seven-day progress.

Bank balance boast

The revelation came in the form of a TikTok video where @sanelycollections09 proudly shared a screenshot of her bank balance. It displayed a substantial income from flipping sought-after kicks from brands like Nike and Adidas.

The video gained significant traction with 298K. The entrepreneur is seen smiling next to stacks of shoe boxes.

Businesswoman builds small house

The woman took her followers on a journey beyond sneakers, revealing that she used her hard-earned profits to build her mother a house. The construction process left netizens amazed at the swift transformation of earnings into a tangible asset.
