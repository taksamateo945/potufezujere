French Bulldog Attacks Brown Bears, Scares Them to Death: Video

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

All bark (and some bite)! One 20-pound French bulldog served as the ultimate security guard to her Monrovia, Calif., home last week. Owner David Hernandez set up security cameras in his yard after bears had been damaging his property, when he noticed something odd. 

In the footage from Friday, Oct. 2, his dog, Jewels, sprinted after two bears who had wandered into a fenced area, attacking them and scaring them off the property. 

Jewels was so vicious that she caused both bears (who easily weighed five times more than she did) to bound away, with one throwing himself back over the fence and out of the yard.
