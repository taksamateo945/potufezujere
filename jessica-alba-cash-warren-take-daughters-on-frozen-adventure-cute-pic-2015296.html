Jessica Alba, Cash Warren Take Daughters on Frozen Adventure: Cute Pic

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, May 21, 2024

A Frozen family affair! Jessica Alba and her husband, Cash Warren, took their daughters Honor, 7, and Haven, 3, to meet Frozen’s Anna, Elsa, and Olaf on Saturday, June 27.

The adorable family of four posed for photos at Disney’s Frozen Summer Fun while vacationing at Walt Disney World Resort in Lake Buena Vista, Florida.

“#letitgo @WaltDisneyWorld #familyvacay @cash_warren @haroldperrineau @markdalba @cathyalba,” the Honest Company founder, 34, captioned a fun group shot on Instagram.

Prior to letting it go, the fam visited The Wizarding World of Harry Potter at Universal Studios Florida on Thursday, June 25. “Family fun day @universalorlando #UniversalStudiosOrlando w @cash_warren @haroldperrineau @markdalba @cathyalba #HarryPotterLand,” Alba captioned another group shot from their kid-approved vacay.

Last week, Alba shared a seriously sweet photo of her little girls wearing color-coordinated dresses and bows in their hair for a Father’s Day photo op with their dad. “Happy Father’s Day to my [heart] @cash_warren,” she gushed alongside her adorable family.

“They’re very girlie,” Alba told Us Weekly earlier this month of her daughters, adding that she tries to keep them entertained with “educational apps.”

“But, of course, the makeover apps are their favorite,” the mom admitted. “It’s like mermaid makeover.”
