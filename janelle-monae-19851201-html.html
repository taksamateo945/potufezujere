Janelle Monáe Robinson is an American singer, songwriter, rapper, actress, record producer and model. Monáe signed a contract with Atlantic Records, as well as for her own brand, the Wondaland Art Association. Monáe received eight Grammy Award nominations. Monáe won the MTV Video Music Awards and ASCAP Vanguard Awards 2010. Monáe also received the Billboard Women in Music Rising Star Award in 2015 and the Winner of the Year Award in 2018. In 2012, Monáe became a spokesperson for CoverGirl . Boston City Council named Janelle Monáe Day in Boston, Massachusetts on October 16, 2013, in recognition of her artistic talent and social leadership. Monáe's musical career began in 2003 with the release of a demo album titled The Audition.
